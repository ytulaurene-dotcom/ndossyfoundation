Who We Are

Fighting Period Poverty, One Girl at a Time

N'dossy Foundation was born from a simple but powerful conviction: period products are not a luxury — they are a necessity. Period poverty forces thousands of girls to miss school, compromise their health, and lose their confidence every month.

Founded by a young woman who decided to fund and provide menstrual hygiene products to those who need them most, N'dossy Foundation operates at the intersection of health equity, education, and women's empowerment.

Learn About Our Work
  • We distribute reusable menstrual pads to girls in under-resourced communities.
  • We run awareness campaigns to destigmatize menstruation and promote menstrual health education.
  • We advocate for policy change to make period products accessible for all girls.
  • We partner with schools, community centers, and local organizations to maximise reach.
  • We believe no girl's education should ever be interrupted by her period.

What We Do

Our Programs

Three pillars of action — products, education, and advocacy — working together to end period poverty.

🩸

Pads4Girls Project

Our flagship initiative: distributing reusable, eco-friendly menstrual pads to girls who cannot afford them. A single donation of 4€ can keep a girl in school for a whole month.

📚

Menstrual Health Education

We go into schools and communities to teach girls and boys about menstrual health, hygiene, and dispel myths — because education is the foundation of dignity.

📢

Period Poverty Advocacy

We raise awareness through campaigns, social media, and community outreach to shift the narrative around menstruation from shame to strength and normalcy.
